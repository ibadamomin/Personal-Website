Fuelmarkt ● live pilot

Founder & Engineer · Jan 2025 — Present · Austin, TX

A fuel intelligence SaaS platform for gas station operators. Independent operators lose thousands in margin every year to manual, error-prone fuel procurement decisions — Fuelmarkt gives them real-time tank data, competitor pricing, and an AI agent that can place purchase orders autonomously.

  • Full-stack app on Next.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL, with the Anthropic API powering the agent layer.
  • Hardware integrations with Veeder-Root TLS-450+ ATGs and OptConnect for tank telemetry, plus competitor retail price intelligence.
  • Interviewed 10+ operators and attended a national industry expo to validate PMF — pivoted from a procurement marketplace to a flat-tier SaaS model on the basis of that feedback.
  • Currently onboarding 2 pilot customers and shipping a Claude tool-use agent that takes decision latency on purchase orders from hours to minutes.
